## üéÜ New Firework Patterns & Styles

### Additional Single Firework Styles

#### 7. **CHRYSANTHEMUM**
- Dense radial burst with **layered petals**
- Sparks form **concentric rings** that expand outward
- Each ring slightly delayed for depth
- Creates a flower-like appearance

#### 8. **PEONY**
- Similar to chrysanthemum but **more compact center**
- **Denser core** with longer outer sparks
- **Asymmetric expansion** for organic feel

#### 9. **SALUTE**
- **Single bright flash** at center
- **No sparks** - just intense light burst
- Very short duration
- Often followed by echo effect

#### 10. **PALM TREE**
- **Upward burst** with **downward trailing sparks**
- Creates a palm tree silhouette
- Strong gravity on trailing sparks

#### 11. **MULTI-BREAK**
- **Primary explosion** followed by **secondary explosions**
- Each secondary at different distances/angles
- Creates cascading effect
- 2-4 breaks per firework

#### 12. **DIADEM**
- **Crown-like pattern** (sparks form upward arc)
- **Central burst** with **upward-curving arms**
- Elegant, symmetrical design

#### 13. **SERPENTINE**

- **Wave-like motion** as sparks travel
- Creates flowing, organic movement

#### 14. **STROBE**
- **Rapid on/off flashing** of sparks
- **Pulsing intensity** throughout lifetime
- Creates stroboscopic effect

#### 15. **COMET**
- **Single bright trail** that travels across screen
- **Long tail** of fading sparks

#### 16. **FISH**
- **Oval burst** with **trailing tail**
- Sparks form fish-like shape

#### 17. **WATERFALL**
- **Cascading downward** sparks
- **Continuous stream** effect

#### 18. **BROCADES**
- **Complex geometric patterns**
- Sparks form **stars, diamonds, or hexagons**
- Multiple layers with different timings

#### 19. **TIME RAIN**
- **Delayed spark ignition** creates **falling rain effect**

#### 20. **BUTTERFLY**
- **Two symmetric wings** expanding outward

## üé® Composite Patterns (Patterns Made of Fireworks)

### Coordinated Multi-Firework Displays

#### 1. **SYMMETRIC BURST**
- **3-5 rockets** launch simultaneously
- Explode in **symmetric pattern** (star, circle, line)
- All explode at **same time** or **cascading sequence**

#### 2. **SEQUENTIAL CASCADE**
- Rockets launch **one after another** in sequence
- Each explosion **triggers next launch** visually
- Creates **wave of explosions** across screen

#### 3. **SPELLING PATTERNS**
- Rockets form **letters or words** when they explode
- Pre-calculated positions for each letter
- Explosions timed to reveal text

#### 4. **GEOMETRIC SHAPES**
- **Heart shape** (2-3 fireworks)
- **Star pattern** (5 fireworks at star points)
- **Circle formation** (8-12 fireworks in circle)
- **Spiral formation** (fireworks along spiral path)

#### 5. **WAVE PATTERN**
- Rockets launch in **wave sequence**
- Explosions create **traveling wave** effect
- Can be horizontal, vertical, or diagonal

#### 6. **CASCADE**
- **Top-to-bottom** or **center-outward** sequence
- Each explosion **slightly delayed**
- Creates **cascading waterfall** of color

#### 7. **SYMMETRIC MIRROR**
- **Two sides** launch simultaneously
- **Mirror image** explosions
- Perfect symmetry

#### 8. **CLUSTER BURST**
- **Multiple rockets** launch to **same point**
- All explode at **slightly different times**
- Creates **dense, layered explosion**

#### 9. **SPIRAL OUTWARD**
- Rockets launch in **spiral pattern**
- Explosions follow **spiral path**
- Creates **rotating display**

#### 10. **RANDOM CLUSTER**
- **Group of rockets** launch to **general area**
- Explosions create **cloud of color**
- Organic, natural appearance

#### 11. **CHAIN REACTION**
- One firework triggers **visual cue** for next
- **Sequential explosions** that appear connected
- Each explosion "points" to next location

#### 12. **GRID PATTERN**
- Rockets launch to **grid positions**
- Explosions form **geometric grid**
- Can be **checkerboard**, **lines**, or **full grid**

#### 13. **FLOWER BLOOM**
- **Center firework** explodes first
- **Petals** (surrounding fireworks) explode after
- Creates **flower opening** effect

#### 14. **FIREWORK TREE**
- **Trunk** (vertical line of explosions)
- **Branches** (horizontal explosions at intervals)
- Creates **tree silhouette**

#### 15. **CONFETTI BURST**
- **Many small fireworks** in **tight cluster**
- All explode **simultaneously**
- Creates **confetti shower** effect

## üöÄ Performance Optimizations

### While Maintaining Visual Quality

#### 1. **LOD System** (Level of Detail)
- **Reduce spark count** when many fireworks active
- **Simplify patterns** at distance
- **Dynamic quality** based on performance

#### 2. **Particle Pooling**
- **Reuse DOM elements** instead of creating new ones
- **Object pooling** for sparks
- **Reduces garbage collection**

#### 3. **Culling**
- **Remove off-screen** sparks early
- **Skip animations** for invisible elements
- **Viewport-based optimization**

#### 4. **Batching**
- **Batch DOM updates** (use DocumentFragment)
- **Reduce reflows** and repaints
- **Optimize CSS transforms**
